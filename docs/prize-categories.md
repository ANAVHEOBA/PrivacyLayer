# Prize Categories & Ideas

> What to build for each prize category.

## Grand Prize

**Best overall privacy application using PrivacyLayer.**

### Winning Characteristics
- Solves a real problem that needs privacy
- Creative use of PrivacyLayer features
- Polished, production-ready feel
- Clear value proposition

### Project Ideas

#### 1. Privacy-Preserving DeFi Dashboard
A DeFi interface where all your positions and transactions are private by default.

**Features:**
- Shielded deposits into AMM pools
- Private yield farming
- Anonymous portfolio tracking
- Stealth withdrawals

**Why it wins:** DeFi is public by default - privacy is a huge differentiator.

#### 2. Anonymous Social Platform
A social network where identity is optional.

**Features:**
- Post anonymously but verifiably
- Private tipping between users
- Anonymous polls and voting
- Reputation without doxxing

**Why it wins:** Social media needs privacy innovation.

#### 3. Confidential Business Payments
B2B payment system with privacy for sensitive transactions.

**Features:**
- Private invoicing
- Shielded payment history
- Anonymous supplier relationships
- Compliance viewing keys for audits

**Why it wins:** Real business use case with clear ROI.

#### 4. Private Charity Platform
Donate to causes without revealing your identity or amount.

**Features:**
- Anonymous donations
- Private fundraising campaigns
- Shielded grant distribution
- Public impact reports with private details

**Why it wins:** Privacy for good - protects donors and recipients.

## Choosing Your Category

### Questions to Ask Yourself

1. **What am I good at?**
   - Frontend → Grand Prize or Real-World Use Case
   - Backend → Developer Experience or Integration
   - Design → Grand Prize or UI/UX
   - Full-stack → Any category

2. **What problem do I care about?**
   - Financial privacy → DeFi or payments
   - Social privacy → Anonymous platform
   - Developer tools → Developer Experience
   - Innovation → Integration or creative use case

3. **What can I build in 24 hours?**
   - Be realistic about scope
   - Focus on one killer feature
   - Polish beats features

### Strategy Tips

- **Don't spread thin:** Pick one category and aim to win it
- **Know the judges:** Research what past winners built
- **Differentiate:** Don't build what everyone else is building
- **Demo matters:** A working demo beats a perfect architecture
